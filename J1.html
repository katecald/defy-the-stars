You hold the dagger aloft with both hands before plunging it deep into your belly. Your vision goes fuzzy as you watch your blood pour onto the stone floor. You die next to Romeo, thinking of how the moon glimmered in his eyes at he gazed up at you from the garden and the way his lips had a way of warming your whole body and the night you spent stroking his hair as you watched him fall asleep.
