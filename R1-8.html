You walk through the empty streets of Verona. The market is closed down. You look up at the stars and cry out. As they twinkle kindly down at you, you rage against them, asking why they would lead you to fall in love with a girl who’d made a vow of chastity before leading you to fall in love with your enemy?

They offer you no answers.

Do you go back and find Juliet?

Do you keep wandering in the night?
