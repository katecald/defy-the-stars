You purchase poison from an apothecary and head back to Verona. You go straight to the tomb where Juliet’s body is so that you can look upon her face when you leave this cruel world.

As you’re breaking into the tomb, you hear someone sneaking up behind you. It’s Paris, the man Juliet’s parents were always encouraging her to marry. He starts yelling at you, accusing you of all sorts of terrible things. He blames you for Juliet’s death, saying she died grieving the loss of her cousin Tybalt. He tells you that you deserve to die. If only he knew how much you agreed with him.

Paris demands a fight.

Do you draw your sword and engage with him?

Do you tell Paris to leave you in peace?
